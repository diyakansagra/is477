## Key Observations

### Genre Comparisons
- Calculated average IMDb rating by IMDb genre.
- Calculated average popularity by TMDB genre.

### Temporal Trends

- Popularity increases sharply for modern films, reflecting TMDB data era.
- Both budget and revenue rise significantly across decades, with strong growth after the 1990s.
